Say hello to the MM1-Pro

The AYRN custom built MM-Pro-1 is designed to deliver the ultimate computing experience. From content creation and media production to gaming and office applications, this workstation delivers the power professionals crave. Each component is carefully selected and fine tuned for exceptional performance out of the box.

Beauty meets functionality

Unparalleled cooling performance comes in a defiant new look. The MM1-Pro features a Swedish designed Fractal Meshify C chasis. The striking black diamond facets and angular asymmetry are sure to carve out a unique place in any office.

Premium graphic capabilities

MM1-Pro features a reference quality Radeon RX 5700 XT 50th Anniversary Edition graphics card with 8 GBs of DDR6 RAM for an incredible and immersive experience.

RDNA Architecture

Radeon RX 5700 XT 50th AE features new compute units better suited for visual effects, and multi-level cache hierarchy for greatly reduced latency and highly responsive gaming.

More Performance, Less Energy

Efficiently energetic, Radeon RX 5700 XT 50th Anniversary delivers more performance while consuming less power.

GDDR6 Memory

Equipped with 8GB of advanced GDDR6 memory to provide high bandwidth of up to 448 GB/s, enabling 1440p performance for today’s most demanding video processes.

Extreme Refresh Rates

Less lag equals more wins. Radeon Anti-Lag reduces latency dramatically, providing ultra-fast response time giving you a dynamic edge over your competition.

Powerhouse processing

 MM-1-Pro is powered by the Ryzen 2700x, one of the most powerful desktop processors on the market.

AMD StoreMI Technology

Enhancing load times, boot times, file management and system responsiveness.

AMD SenseMI Technology

True machine intelligence and accelerated system performance right at your fingertips.
